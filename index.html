UMD ACM Robot

I applied for and received a UROP grant to do research on the ACM Club's robot during Spring 2019. The research explored improving the wifi connection that was used to remotely control the robot. The robot had hardware failures during the course of the research that added more repair time than I had budgeted, so the project was extended to Fall 2019, with a final presentation given shortly before I graduated.
